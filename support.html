Common Questions

How do I track a restaurant?

Tap the "+" button on the map view to add a new restaurant. Fill in the details and save!

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

The free plan lets you save up to 20 restaurants. Premium unlocks unlimited saves and enhanced features.

How do I mark a restaurant as visited?

Open the restaurant details and toggle the "Visited" status. You can add photos, ratings, and notes too!
